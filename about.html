Our Story

Bridging Clinical Science & Patient Care

LDRGLPRx was founded on a simple but powerful idea: the most effective weight loss treatments in modern medicine shouldn't be locked behind long wait times, prohibitive costs, or geographic limitations.

Our founders — physicians who spent years treating obesity in clinical settings — saw firsthand how GLP-1 receptor agonists were transforming patient outcomes. They also saw how many people who could benefit from these medications simply couldn't access them.

That gap became our mission. By combining board-certified medical expertise with a streamlined telehealth platform, we built LDRGLPRx to bring physician-guided GLP-1 treatment directly to patients across all 50 states — safely, affordably, and on their schedule.
