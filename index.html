Understanding GLP-1

What Are GLP-1 Medications?

GLP-1 receptor agonists are a class of medications that mimic the incretin hormone GLP-1, which your body naturally produces after eating. They work by regulating appetite, slowing gastric emptying, and improving blood sugar control.

Originally developed to treat Type 2 diabetes, these medications have shown remarkable efficacy for weight management when combined with lifestyle modifications and physician oversight.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about GLP-1 weight loss treatment with LDRGLPRx.

GLP-1 medications are typically prescribed for adults with a BMI of 30 or greater, or a BMI of 27+ with at least one weight-related health condition (such as high blood pressure, type 2 diabetes, or high cholesterol). Your physician will determine eligibility during your telehealth consultation based on your complete health profile.

The active ingredients (semaglutide, tirzepatide) are FDA-approved. Our compounded formulations are prepared by licensed pharmacies following FDA regulations. Compounded medications are not individually FDA-approved but are legally prescribed and dispensed when a licensed provider determines they are medically appropriate.

The most common side effects include nausea, diarrhea, constipation, and reduced appetite, particularly during dose titration. These typically lessen over time. Your physician will start you on a low dose and gradually increase it to minimize side effects. Your care team is available 24/7 to address any concerns.

After completing your online health assessment, you'll be scheduled for a secure video consultation with a licensed physician. They'll review your medical history, discuss your goals, and determine if GLP-1 therapy is appropriate for you. The entire process is HIPAA-compliant and can be done from the comfort of your home.

Once your physician issues a prescription, it's typically filled and shipped within 3-5 business days. Medications are shipped in temperature-controlled packaging to ensure quality. You'll receive tracking information as soon as your order ships.
